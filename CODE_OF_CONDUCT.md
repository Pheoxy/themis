# Code Of Conduct

Themis exists to protect maintainers and contributors from avoidable review burden. Project discussion should follow the same goal.

Expected behavior:

- Be direct, factual, and respectful.
- Assume contributors may be new, but do not lower quality requirements.
- Critique code, evidence, and process instead of attacking people.
- Disclose AI assistance honestly when it affected submitted work.
- Respect maintainer decisions about scope, risk, and review bandwidth.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Pressuring maintainers to accept unreviewable or untested work.
- Hiding generated content, copied code, or licensing uncertainty.
- Repeatedly submitting low-effort changes after being told what evidence is required.

Maintainers may close issues, lock discussions, or block participants to protect project health.
